                <h2>How do I buy a domain?</h2>

                <p>Yes. Use the “Make an Offer” form on the domain page. We may accept, counter, or decline at our discretion.</p>
            </div>
            <div class="content-card">
                <h2>What payment methods are accepted?</h2>
                <p>Payment options are provided by our payment partner (e.g., Escrow.com) and may include credit card, wire transfer, or other approved methods. Availability can vary by region and transaction size.</p>
            </div>
            <div class="content-card">
                <h2>Are there extra fees?</h2>
                <p>Registrar, transfer, or escrow fees may apply depending on your registrar and payment method. Taxes and bank fees are your responsibility where applicable.</p>
            </div>

                <p>Most transfers complete within a few days after payment clears, depending on the registrar. We start the transfer promptly and guide you on any steps required on your side.</p>
            </div>
